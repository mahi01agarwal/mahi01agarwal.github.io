## Research Experience

### Reservoir Computing & Hyperchaotic Systems
**Role:** Research Intern
**Status:** Under review at *Springer – Multimedia Tools and Applications*



* Developed an advanced **image encryption** scheme using **RC models** to enhance security and efficiency in transmitting visual data.
* Integrated **chaos theory** and **fractional-order systems** to improve encryption for real-time applications.
* Applied **RC-generated chaotic sequences** for diffusion, ensuring high unpredictability throughout encryption.
* Utilized a **two-key system**: the first key generates system parameters (**rho**, **phi**), while the second controls chaotic diffusion for enhanced randomness.
* Implemented **pixel shuffling** and **bitwise XOR operations** to scramble pixel values, followed by reshaping into the original image format.
* Validated encryption robustness against **differential attacks** using **NPCR, UACI, and histogram variance metrics**.

### IIIT Delhi — Hand Gesture Occlusion Research Project
**Role:** C4GT Intern
**Duration:** Jun 2025 – Aug 2025



* Built a scalable pipeline to extract **3D hand landmarks** from RGB images and compare gestures using **point cloud similarity metrics**.
* Used **MediaPipe** for landmark detection, **Open3D** for point cloud generation/export, and **PyTorch3D** for computing **Chamfer Distance**.
* Enabled **parallel GPU-accelerated processing** for efficient computation on large gesture datasets.
* Visualized 3D hand landmarks and analyzed gesture similarity patterns using histograms and qualitative inspection.
